Erika Jaquez
Erika Jaquez is a designer and painter currently based in Los Angeles working at Another Studio. She graduated from Parsons School of Design where she earned her BFA in Communication Design in 2019. She has previously interned with The MP Shift, Marvel Entertainment, and DeVries Global. Erika is currently available for freelance design work, and painting commissions.
