ERIKA JAQUEZ
Catalog 01, designed in collaboration with Mercy de la Cruz and Karen Mizrahi, examines different tools that we use to create music and art. Through the juxtaposition of imagery, we come to a new understanding of tools and how they relate to the process of creation. The catalog focuses on various themes and visuals, including but not limited to, childhood, music equipment, art supplies, physicality, sound, etc.
